ABOUT

AAPI (Asian American Pacific Islander) is an organization & community created as a response to the alarming escalation in xenophobia & bigotry resulting from the COVID-19 pandemic. Our mission is to raise awareness & provide support to those who have been impacted by the pandemic. We also strive to support local small businesses & independent artists/designers. All proceeds raised from our events will go toward the Stop Asian Hate campaign.
