Expressions in Films

This data collection focuses on facial expressions caught in famous films and/or t.v. shows throughout the years. It will be catagorized by moods, emotions, and year it was produced. The overall design of the website will replicate a vintage film reel projection. Films that will be featured include: As Tears Go By (1988), The Days of Being Wild (1990), Chungking Express (1994), Forest Gump (1994), Fallen Angels (1995), Happy Together (1997), In the Mood for Love (2000), American Psycho (2000), The Pursuit of Happiness (2006), The Boy in the Striped Pajamas (2008), Twillight (2009), Guernsey Literary and Potato Peel Pie Society (2018), The Umbrella Academy (2019), and Parasite (2019).
